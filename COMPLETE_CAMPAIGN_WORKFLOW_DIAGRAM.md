## 🎯 **WORKFLOW SUMMARY**

### **10 PHASES - COMPLETE CAMPAIGN LIFECYCLE:**

1. **🔍 Discovery** - Staff finds influencers via Modash API
2. **👥 Shortlist** - Brands browse and shortlist influencers  
3. **📝 Quotation** - Brands request campaign quotes
4. **✅ Approval** - Brands approve staff quotations
5. **🚀 Creation** - System auto-creates campaigns
6. **📞 Outreach** - Staff manually contacts influencers
7. **🎬 Content** - Influencers create and submit content
8. **📊 Review** - Staff reviews and approves content
9. **💰 Payment** - Automated payment processing
10. **📈 Analytics** - Performance tracking and reporting

### **KEY INTEGRATION POINTS:**
- ✅ **Real-time synchronization** across all portals
- ✅ **Automated status updates** throughout workflow
- ✅ **Complete audit trail** from discovery to payment
- ✅ **Role-based access control** at every step
- ✅ **External API integration** with Modash analytics

**This workflow handles everything from initial influencer discovery to final payment processing with complete transparency and automation where appropriate.**
